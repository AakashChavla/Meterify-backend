Your SaaS product will:

* Summarize monthly API call counts automatically

* Allow small startups to track and monetize their APIs easily

   * api\_key

   * created\_at

2. **api\_usage\_logs**

   * id (Primary Key)
   * user\_id (Foreign Key â†’ users.id)

3. **monthly\_usage\_summary**

   * id
